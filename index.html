27% of coral reefs are already dead from human activity. Another 51% will die within our lifetimes unless we make a change.

The largest threats to coral reefs are global warming and ocean acidification. These problems are related to our total greenhouse gas emissions. This carbon footprint calculator estimates how many tons of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases your choices create each year, so you may be more informed on your influence on the health of world coral reefs.
